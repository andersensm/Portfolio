About Me

I was born in Nebraska, but I grew up in the Northern Virginia Area. I was lucky enough to have lots of family close by that I could spend time with, and the rest of my extended family is speard out across the United States. As I grew up, I loved to play soccer with my friends and Virginia's organized soccer leagues; Go Kiwanis!!. I went to school as a child in Arlington, VA. I recieved my Associate degree in General Studies from Northern Virginia Community College. I got my Bachelor's degree at George Mason University with a major in Geography and minor in Geographic Information Systems. I have spent the last 6 years working as a Geospatial Analyst for Leidos as a federal contractor. I have been expanding my skillset into Web Development attending a 6-month program at George Washington University.

I got married in 2012 to the most fantastic person I've ever met! We had a destination wedding in the Dominican Republic and went on an all inclusive cruise around the caribbean, it was so special for us! Since then we have expanded our family to include two beautiful girls; 1 and 3 years old. We love to go to the beach in the summer. We make sand castles, go boogey boarding, fly kites, and snorkle. About every two years, as a family, we go to Disney World in Orlando, FL. and we have the time of our lives. When we aren't spending time on vacation, we hang out with family and friends from our neighborhood. When family and friends aren't around, I enjoy coding and learning new things!
